About Me

My name is Georgios Panagopoulos and I'm a graduate student at the School of Applied Mathematical and Physical Sciences (SAMPS) of the National Technical University of Athens (NTUA) in the program "Physics and Technological Applications". My academic interests lie in various topics in Theoretical and Mathematical Physics.

I am a member of the EPS Young Minds Section of NTUA, as well as the Hellenic Society for the Science and Technology of Condensed Matter. Additionally, I am one of the moderators of the SAMPS' students Google Drive.

Finally, I have been elected representative of the students in the General Assembly of SAMPS for the years 2023 and 2025.
